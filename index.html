Palimpsest

An open observatory that measures how authoritarian states censor by treating deletion as data, and now measures how their state-aligned AI models refuse and rewrite the truth. It watches the censor, never the censored.

The Generative Firewall Index

China regulates what its AI models are allowed to say. This is a live, updating index of what a state's AI is engineered to hide: the share of sensitive answers that state-aligned models refuse or rewrite, with neutral controls to prove the suppression is deliberate and selective.

View the live index → Raw evidence (JSON)

What it does

  • Deletion as data. Archives public posts, watches for their scrubbing, and ranks what the censor is most focused on right now. Validated against six documented censorship events.
  • The generation layer. Probes state-aligned LLMs and records their refusals and state-narrative substitutions, in Chinese and English, against Western controls.
  • Safety as architecture. Public reads only, no person inside is ever asked to act, and no state-aligned model is ever the analyst. Every finding ships its raw evidence.
