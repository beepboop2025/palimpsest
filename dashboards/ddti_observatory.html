Provenance · how this score was derived
Method: threat = attention × (1 + 1.5 · novelty). Scope: censor-attention allocation (numerator-only; not a true deletion rate). Source: China Digital Times curated deletion stream.
Safety: public content only · never deanonymises posters or contributors · deletion confirmed by multiple independent checks · signal suppressed when the network is unreliable.
